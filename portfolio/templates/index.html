About Me

I am Patience Aanuoluwapo Ajimuda, a final year student of Obafemi Awolowo University studying English Education, I am an educationist and also a passionate entrepreneur.
I have goals targeted at changing the feminine world as regarding gender inequality and child education. I currently volunteer in an NGO where we are passionate about developing children, raising children and supporting those who needs help in financing their education.
I am a lover of Christ and His body. I aim to become a writer as the years go by thereby influencing the minds of young men and women positively. I am a member of All Nigerian United Nations Student Association (ANUNSA). I have passion for cooking and anything that has to do with the kitchen and the table. I love making new recipes and trying new cooking ideas. Cooking is just a part of me.
